## What to Consider Before Buying in 2026

1.  **MPPT vs. PWM:** Always choose an MPPT (Maximum Power Point Tracking) controller if your budget allows. It can increase your solar harvest by up to 30%.
2.  **Mounting Hardware:** Ensure the kit includes Z-brackets or mounting feet compatible with your roof type (metal, fiberglass, or shingle).
3.  **Battery Compatibility:** Most kits work with Lead-Acid, Gel, and LiFePO4 batteries, but always double-check the charge controller settings.

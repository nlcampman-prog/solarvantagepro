## Buyer's Guide: How to Choose Your Unit

When shopping for a solar generator in 2026, focus on these three critical factors:

### Capacity vs. Mobility
Capacity (Wh) determines how long the battery lasts, while Mobility depends on weight. If you plan to move the unit frequently, don't exceed 50 lbs. If it’s staying in your garage for backup, prioritize the highest Wh you can afford.

### The LiFePO4 Advantage
Every unit we recommend uses LiFePO4 batteries. Avoid older Lithium-Ion (NMC) units. LiFePO4 is safer, non-combustible, and offers up to 10 times the cycle life of traditional lithium batteries.

### UPS Functionality
A "True UPS" (Uninterruptible Power Supply) means the generator can stay plugged into the wall and your devices. When the grid fails, the generator takes over in under 20 milliseconds. This is a "must-have" feature for 2026 if you work from home.

## Performance & Usability

- **Charging Speed:** EcoFlow wins here. Their X-Stream technology allows the Delta Pro to charge from 0-80% in under 90 minutes. Lipower, given its massive capacity, takes longer but supports higher solar input to compensate.
- **Output:** The Lipower 6144Wh system offers a slightly higher continuous AC output (4000W), making it better for running power-hungry items like well pumps or central AC units during an emergency.
- **Portability:** Neither of these are "lightweight," but the EcoFlow Delta Pro features built-in wheels and a telescoping handle that makes moving it around the house significantly easier than the Lipower unit.

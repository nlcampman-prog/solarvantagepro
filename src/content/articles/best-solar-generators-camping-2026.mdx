## What to Consider for a Camping Generator

When choosing a power station for the outdoors, focus on these three things:
1.  **Portability:** Can you easily carry it from the car to the campsite?
2.  **Capacity (Wh):** Do you need just enough for phones (300Wh) or enough for a weekend of appliances (1000Wh+)?
3.  **Durability:** Look for rugged builds and high-quality LiFePO4 batteries that can handle the bumps of the road.

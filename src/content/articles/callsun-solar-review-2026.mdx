## Industry-Leading Efficiency: The N-Type Advantage

The most surprising feature of CallSun’s 2026 lineup is the use of **N-Type solar cells**. Typically reserved for premium rooftop installations or high-end portable panels, N-Type cells offer superior performance in low-light conditions and better long-term durability against LID (Light Induced Degradation).

CallSun panels boast an efficiency rating of up to **25%**. To put that in perspective:
- **CallSun:** ~25% efficiency
- **Jackery SolarSaga:** ~23-24% efficiency
- **EcoFlow Portable Panels:** ~22-23% efficiency

This means you’re getting more power per square inch, allowing you to charge your power stations faster even on slightly overcast days.

Budget panels often cut corners on build quality, but CallSun has invested in the right places.

- **ETFE Coating:** Their portable panels use ETFE (Ethylene Tetrafluoroethylene) rather than cheaper PET. ETFE is more durable, easier to clean, and has better light transmission.

- **Universal Compatibility:** CallSun panels typically come with a multi-connector cable (including DC8011, XT60, and Anderson), making them compatible with almost every major power station on the market, from Jackery to Bluetti.
